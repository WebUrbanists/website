Financial Support to Help run the Instance

As a community-owned and operated platform, we rely on the support of our users to continue providing a positive and inclusive space for discussions and connections. We are currently accepting financial contributions from our users to help us maintain and improve our platform. Your contributions will go towards things like hosting costs, technical support, and community engagement initiatives. By financially supporting WebUrbanists, you are not only helping to keep our platform running, but you are also supporting a community-owned alternative to large, commercial social media platforms. Your contributions will help us continue to provide a positive and engaging space for urbanists and city enthusiasts to connect and share ideas. If you are able, we encourage you to consider making a financial contribution to WebUrbanists. Every little bit helps, and we are grateful for your support. You can find more information about how to contribute on our website. Thank you for being a part of the WebUrbanists community, and for your support in keeping our platform running.
